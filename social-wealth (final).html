The Proven Path

Your Six-Figure Creator Roadmap

Five sequential phases. Each unlocks the next. Follow in order for the fastest path to consistent monthly revenue.

01
Foundation

Build a Niche Brand & Website

Choose a specific, high-value niche and launch a branded website. Set up your content hub with SEO-optimized pages, an email capture, and linked social profiles. Your site is your monetization engine.

02
Audience

Grow an Engaged Audience

Post consistently across 1–2 platforms. Prioritize engagement rate over follower count. Aim for 10,000+ followers with 4%+ engagement before activating paid monetization — quality over quantity.

03
Monetize

Activate AdSense & Affiliates

Enable Google AdSense on your website and join 3–5 high-ticket affiliate programs relevant to your niche. Route social traffic to content pages with embedded affiliate links for passive income stacking.

04
Scale

Land Brand Sponsorships

Create a professional media kit with audience demographics, engagement stats, and past results. Pitch 10 brands per week. Start with micro-deals ($500–$2k) to build your sponsorship portfolio and testimonials.

05
Compound

Launch Your Digital Product

Productize your expertise into a course, template pack, or membership. Launch to your email list first with a Beta offer at 50% discount. Use testimonials to fuel your full public launch at full price.
